Frequently asked questions

What services does your company provide?

We provide services for the transportation of people and their luggage from Ukraine to EU countries and vice versa. In addition, we offer delivery of parcels, transportation of medicines and documents. There are also individual transfer services (rent a bus with a driver).

Where are you leaving from?

In Ukraine, we arrive and depart from railway or bus stations. In Europe, we deliver and pick you up from the specified address.

Do you accept cashless payment?

Yes, we accept cashless payment for our services. Convenient payment without cash is one of our possibilities.

Are discounts possible?

Yes, we offer discounts for children and senior citizens. Please contact our support team for more information and to take advantage of these discounts.

What is included in the price of the trip?

The price of the trip includes transportation of one passenger, one suitcase and hand luggage. There is also free still water and wireless internet.

How can I book a trip?

Booking a trip is very easy. Contact our customer support service by phone or write to us in any messenger that is convenient for you. Cost reservation 10% of the ticket price.

Can I hand over the package?

Yes, we transport parcels, documents, medicines. Check the price with the operator.

Is it possible to order an individual transfer?

Yes, call us or use the feedback form to find out the details, determine the date and find out the price of the service.
